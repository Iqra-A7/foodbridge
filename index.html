Process

How FoodBridge Works

Four simple steps turn surplus food into community nourishment.

🏪
1

List Surplus

Restaurants & stores post available food with pickup windows on the app or portal.

📲
2

Match & Alert

Our smart system matches food with the nearest verified charities and notifies volunteers.

🚴
3

Pickup & Deliver

Volunteers collect food and deliver it safely to partnered shelters and community kitchens.

💚
4

Impact Tracked

Every donation is tracked — donors receive real-time impact reports and sustainability badges.

Features

Everything You Need to Give & Get

Purpose-built tools for donors, volunteers, and charities alike.

🗺️

Live Food Map

See every available donation on an interactive map. Filter by food type, distance, and pickup time.

🤖

AI Smart Matching

Our AI pairs surplus with the highest-need charities, minimising travel time and maximising freshness.

📊

Impact Dashboard

Donors and charities get real-time dashboards — meals donated, CO₂ prevented, money saved.

🔔

Instant Alerts

Push notifications and SMS alerts keep volunteers and charities informed within minutes of a listing.

🏅

Badges & Rewards

Gamified milestones motivate donors and volunteers. Earn Eco Hero, Community Star, and more.

🔐

Safe & Verified

All donors, volunteers, and charities are ID-verified. Food safety logs are kept for every transaction.
